What happens after I buy a license?

You will receive 2 emails: a receipt for your transaction and a separate email with the license activation code.

What if I move to another computer?

You can manage license activations inside the app and deactivate unused devices.

What if I changed my mind?

No questions asked refund for up to 2 months use for Pro license and pro-rata refund for yearly Pro+.
